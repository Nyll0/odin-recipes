High Landers Potato Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients
  • 1/2 KG potatoes
  • 1/2 spoon of all purpose flour
  • 1/4 Onion
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt
  • 5 spoons of vegetable oil
  • For garnish heavy cream & parsly
Recipe
  1. Peel the potatoes and grate them on a small grater. hold them for about 5 minutes without stirring and then hold them in your hands thoroughly before applying the juice. For the squeezed wires, finely grate an egg and two pinches of salt, dangerous.
  2. Heat a frying pan (20-22 cm diameter), pour 3 tablespoons of oil. Pour the entire potato mass into the heated oil and spread it into an even pancake. Fry over medium heat for approx. 4 minutes until golden, turn over, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of oil and repeat frying (approx. 3 minutes).
  3. Place on a plate, add hot goulash and fold in half. Pour thick cream on top and sprinkle with parsley.
